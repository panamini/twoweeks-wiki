# Logo System

The product logo system centers on `two weeks.` with a terracotta period. Compact surfaces use `tw.`; AI work uses the same period as the only ambient motion signal.

## Current state

| Context | Mark |
| --- | --- |
| Full app header | `two weeks.` |
| Collapsed sidebar / compact nav | `tw.` |
| App icon | `tw.` |
| AI work | breathing terracotta period |
| Campaign | `TWO WEEKS. PERIOD.` |

## Rules

- Product UI uses the wordmark, not a tagline.
- `tw.` is the collapsed logo and icon state.
- The period is terracotta and may breathe only during AI work.
- The `II` pause motif stays for marketing/campaign contexts, not product UI.
- Do not animate the favicon.

## Copy layers

- Logo: `two weeks.`
- Brand tagline: `Finish. Faster.`
- Campaign punch: `Just quit.`
- Product promise: `One click. Two weeks. Done.`
