# UI Design Audit Prompt

## Summary

Structured high-stakes UI design audit prompt for reviewing screenshots against premium 2026 product standards.

## Key points

- Judge only visible evidence and say when static screenshots cannot prove a behavior.
- Separate objective craft problems, accessibility risks, UX risks, and subjective taste observations.
- Score light mode, dark mode, category quality, and overall readiness.
- Require severity labels, confidence labels, exact fixes, and token/system recommendations.
- Be especially critical of dark mode and avoid confusing trendiness with quality.
