# Twoweeks.ai Motion System

## Summary

Full motion-system audit and spec defining Twoweeks motion as proof, causality, and final settle rather than decorative movement.

## Key points

- Motion thesis: the point thinks, the document changes, the interface proves it, then everything settles.
- The terracotta period is the only permitted ambient loop and only during AI work.
- Required primitives include breathing dot, stage text/progress steps, region flash, diff reveal, success settle, error contour, active rail, and non-shimmer skeleton-to-content.
- AI work must use stage text and source/proof signals, not spinners, sparkles, shimmer, typing dots, or per-character animation.
- Reduced motion, accessibility, color independence, and performance constraints are first-class.
