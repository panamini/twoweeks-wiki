# Brand Logo System

## Summary

Note defining the practical logo system: `two weeks.` for full app, `tw.` for compact states, terracotta period for AI work, and campaign-specific uses.

## Key points

- Full app header: `two weeks.`
- Collapsed sidebar/app icon: `tw.`
- AI state: breathing terracotta period.
- Website/campaign can use `Two weeks. Period.` / `TWO WEEKS. PERIOD.`
- Product UI should avoid tagline clutter.

## Implications

Creates [[design/logo-system]] and aligns logo motion with [[design/motion-system]].
