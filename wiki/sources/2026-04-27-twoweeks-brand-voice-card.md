# Twoweeks.ai Brand Voice Card

## Summary

Compact brand voice card defining Twoweeks as declarative, compressed, anti-corporate, and leisure-forward.

## Key points

- Archetype: anti-corporate productivity coach.
- UI labels use Title Case for nouns and short verb-first commands.
- Marketing can use all-caps two-word panel heads with terminal periods.
- Success toasts are one word; failure toasts are two to three words.
- Avoid exclamation, ellipses, em-dash headlines, corporate filler, hype, and coach-voice empathy.
- User-facing noun locks favor `resume`, `cover letter`, and `Twoweeks`.
