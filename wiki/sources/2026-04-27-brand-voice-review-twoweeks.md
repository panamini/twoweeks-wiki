# Brand Voice Review — Twoweeks.ai

## Summary

Review of marketing panels and values copy against the Twoweeks voice profile.

## Key points

- Protect the `X. Y.` two-period panel-head rhythm.
- Strongest brand moves are the period rhythm and the "Elite Indifference" / Friday-afternoon juxtaposition.
- Fixes called out: avoid `Zero fluff`, `Not X, just Y`, `You got this`, `Unrivaled`, and `Powerful under the hood`.
- The voice should stay declarative, compressed, and anti-corporate without drifting into generic hype.

## Implications

Reinforces [[design/brand-voice]] and its banned construction list.
