

# ATS Excellence Audit Form for a CV/Résumé

Use this as the **benchmark form** for another LLM.

## Scoring scale

- **95–100** = excellent / ATS-safe / recruiter-ready
    
- **85–94** = strong, minor risks
    
- **70–84** = usable but risky
    
- **Below 70** = likely ATS problems or weak match
    

## Automatic-fail blockers

If **any** of these are true, cap the total score at **69** until fixed:

- Resume is a **scanned or image-based PDF** or text cannot be cleanly copied out in reading order. ([Oleeo](https://www.oleeo.com/blog/what-is-an-applicant-tracking-system-ats/ "What is an Applicant Tracking System (ATS)? A Full 2025 Guide"))
    
- Important content is placed in **tables, text boxes, headers, footers, sidebars, or multi-column layout**. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/200989175-Unsuccessful-resume-parse "Unsuccessful resume parse – Greenhouse Support"))
    
- Resume uses **graphics, icons, charts, photos, logos, word art, progress bars, or heavy design elements** to communicate important information. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/200989175-Unsuccessful-resume-parse "Unsuccessful resume parse – Greenhouse Support"))
    
- File format **violates the application instructions** or is unsupported by the portal. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/360052218132-Supported-formats-for-resumes-cover-letters-and-other-candidate-uploads "Supported formats for resumes, cover letters and other candidate uploads – Greenhouse Support"))
    
- Standard resume sections are missing or labeled with **creative/nonstandard headings** the parser may not map correctly. ([Utah Valley University](https://www.uvu.edu/career-internship/docs/career-services/creating-ats-friendly-resumes.pdf "Crack the Code: Creating ATS Friendly Resumes"))
    
- Contact info is hidden in a **header/footer/text box**. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/200989175-Unsuccessful-resume-parse "Unsuccessful resume parse – Greenhouse Support"))
    
- There are **serious spelling/grammar errors** in names, titles, skills, certifications, or dates. ([Utah Valley University](https://www.uvu.edu/career-internship/docs/career-services/creating-ats-friendly-resumes.pdf "Crack the Code: Creating ATS Friendly Resumes"))

# Weighted rubric (100 points)

## 1) File format and parse integrity — 20 points

Score each item exactly as written.

- **5 pts**: File format follows the employer’s requested format; if none is specified, resume is delivered as **DOCX or text-based PDF**, not Pages, image file, or scan. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/360052218132-Supported-formats-for-resumes-cover-letters-and-other-candidate-uploads "Supported formats for resumes, cover letters and other candidate uploads – Greenhouse Support"))
    
- **5 pts**: Text is fully selectable and copy-pastes in the correct order with no corruption, missing bullets, random symbols, or jumbled sections. ([Utah Valley University](https://www.uvu.edu/career-internship/docs/career-services/creating-ats-friendly-resumes.pdf "Crack the Code: Creating ATS Friendly Resumes"))
    
- **5 pts**: File is lightweight and not image-heavy; oversized files can break parsing. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/200989175-Unsuccessful-resume-parse "Unsuccessful resume parse – Greenhouse Support"))
    
- **5 pts**: Filename is professional and clear, such as `FirstName_LastName_Resume`. This is a best-practice presentation criterion rather than a parser rule, but it supports professional handling. ([Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume-template "How To Write an ATS Resume (With Template and Tips) | Indeed.com"))
    

## 2) Layout and graphic design safety — 20 points

This is the core “graphic design” section.

- **5 pts**: Layout is **single-column**. No sidebars. No split content. No left/right narrative columns. ([Microsoft Word Online](https://word.cloud.microsoft/create/en/blog/best-resume-fonts/ "The best resume fonts, sizes, and formatting tips (2026) | Microsoft Word Blog"))
    
- **5 pts**: No tables, text boxes, shapes, charts, icons, photos, logos, banners, or infographic elements. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/200989175-Unsuccessful-resume-parse "Unsuccessful resume parse – Greenhouse Support"))
    
- **5 pts**: Contact details and core content are in the **main body**, not header/footer. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/200989175-Unsuccessful-resume-parse "Unsuccessful resume parse – Greenhouse Support"))
    
- **5 pts**: Visual hierarchy is simple and machine-safe: standard margins, consistent spacing, dark text on light background, no decorative styling that carries meaning. Princeton recommends **0.75–1 inch margins** and no less than **0.5**; Microsoft recommends a single-column layout, standard margins, and consistent spacing. ([Center for Career Development](https://careerdevelopment.princeton.edu/resume-guide/basic-principles-resume-writing "Basic Principles of Resume Writing | Center for Career Development"))
    

## 3) Typography and readability — 15 points

This is the “typo / typography” section.

- **5 pts**: Uses standard, readable fonts such as **Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman**; body text is usually **10–12 pt** and headings are clearly larger. ([Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume-template "How To Write an ATS Resume (With Template and Tips) | Indeed.com"))
    
- **5 pts**: Uses no more than one primary body font and minimal style variation; no decorative fonts, no inconsistent font switching. ([Microsoft Word Online](https://word.cloud.microsoft/create/en/blog/best-resume-fonts/ "The best resume fonts, sizes, and formatting tips (2026) | Microsoft Word Blog"))
    
- **5 pts**: Avoids underline and excessive italics for important information; uses bold, spacing, and standard heading styles for emphasis instead. ([Microsoft Word Online](https://word.cloud.microsoft/create/en/blog/best-resume-fonts/ "The best resume fonts, sizes, and formatting tips (2026) | Microsoft Word Blog"))
    

## 4) Section structure and data extraction clarity — 20 points

This is about how well the ATS can map the resume into fields.

- **5 pts**: Uses standard headings like **Summary / Professional Summary, Skills, Work Experience, Education, Certifications, Projects, Volunteer Experience**. Do not use creative titles like “My Journey” or “Where I’ve Made Impact.” ([Utah Valley University](https://www.uvu.edu/career-internship/docs/career-services/creating-ats-friendly-resumes.pdf "Crack the Code: Creating ATS Friendly Resumes"))
    
- **5 pts**: Work history is in **reverse chronological order** and each role clearly shows **job title, employer, dates**, and optionally location. ([Center for Career Development](https://careerdevelopment.princeton.edu/resume-guide/basic-principles-resume-writing "Basic Principles of Resume Writing | Center for Career Development"))
    
- **5 pts**: Job titles and company names are written clearly and fully; avoid vague abbreviations where they may weaken parsing. Greenhouse specifically notes better parsing with clear company identifiers and spelled-out titles. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/200989175-Unsuccessful-resume-parse "Unsuccessful resume parse – Greenhouse Support"))
    
- **5 pts**: Education entries clearly show **degree, field, institution, and date** in a standard format. ([Utah Valley University](https://www.uvu.edu/career-internship/docs/career-services/creating-ats-friendly-resumes.pdf "Crack the Code: Creating ATS Friendly Resumes"))
    

## 5) Keyword alignment to the target role — 15 points

This is the part that most affects ranking/match.

- **5 pts**: Resume includes the **exact keywords and phrases** used in the job description for skills, tools, certifications, functions, and role terms. ([Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume-template "How To Write an ATS Resume (With Template and Tips) | Indeed.com"))
    
- **5 pts**: Keywords are placed in the right places: **summary, skills, and experience bullets**, not dumped into an isolated block only. ([Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume-template "How To Write an ATS Resume (With Template and Tips) | Indeed.com"))
    
- **5 pts**: Includes both **full term + acronym** where relevant, for example `Certified Public Accountant (CPA)` or `Master of Business Administration (MBA)`. Avoid keyword stuffing or hidden text. ([Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume-template "How To Write an ATS Resume (With Template and Tips) | Indeed.com"))
    

## 6) Content quality and recruiter readability — 10 points

ATS compatibility is not enough; the resume must still persuade a human.

- **4 pts**: Bullets are concise and achievement-focused, not vague job-duty paragraphs. Princeton recommends bullets rather than dense paragraphs. ([Center for Career Development](https://careerdevelopment.princeton.edu/resume-guide/basic-principles-resume-writing "Basic Principles of Resume Writing | Center for Career Development"))
    
- **3 pts**: Bullets begin with strong action verbs and show outcomes/results where possible. ([Utah Valley University](https://www.uvu.edu/career-internship/docs/career-services/creating-ats-friendly-resumes.pdf "Crack the Code: Creating ATS Friendly Resumes"))
    
- **3 pts**: Content is relevant to the target role and not overloaded with unrelated history. This is a synthesis benchmark based on ATS keyword guidance and recruiter readability guidance. ([Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume-template "How To Write an ATS Resume (With Template and Tips) | Indeed.com"))

# Detailed audit checklist the LLM should apply

## A. Parser-safe design rules

Mark **Pass / Fail / Warning** for each.

- Single-column only. ([Microsoft Word Online](https://word.cloud.microsoft/create/en/blog/best-resume-fonts/ "The best resume fonts, sizes, and formatting tips (2026) | Microsoft Word Blog"))
    
- No tables. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/200989175-Unsuccessful-resume-parse "Unsuccessful resume parse – Greenhouse Support"))
    
- No text boxes. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/200989175-Unsuccessful-resume-parse "Unsuccessful resume parse – Greenhouse Support"))
    
- No headers or footers for important data. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/200989175-Unsuccessful-resume-parse "Unsuccessful resume parse – Greenhouse Support"))
    
- No columns or sidebar layouts. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/200989175-Unsuccessful-resume-parse "Unsuccessful resume parse – Greenhouse Support"))
    
- No icons, logos, photos, charts, graphics, or infographic rating bars. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/200989175-Unsuccessful-resume-parse "Unsuccessful resume parse – Greenhouse Support"))
    
- No scanned/image PDF. ([Oleeo](https://www.oleeo.com/blog/what-is-an-applicant-tracking-system-ats/ "What is an Applicant Tracking System (ATS)? A Full 2025 Guide"))
    
- No Canva-style exported graphic resume for ATS upload. Recent university guidance explicitly warns these can parse as one large text box or lose order/characters. ([Utah Valley University](https://www.uvu.edu/career-internship/docs/career-services/creating-ats-friendly-resumes.pdf "Crack the Code: Creating ATS Friendly Resumes"))
    

## B. Typography rules

- Body text 10–12 pt. ([Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume-template "How To Write an ATS Resume (With Template and Tips) | Indeed.com"))
    
- Headings larger than body, usually 12–14 pt. ([Microsoft Word Online](https://word.cloud.microsoft/create/en/blog/best-resume-fonts/ "The best resume fonts, sizes, and formatting tips (2026) | Microsoft Word Blog"))
    
- Standard fonts only. ([Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume-template "How To Write an ATS Resume (With Template and Tips) | Indeed.com"))
    
- Limit font variation. ([Microsoft Word Online](https://word.cloud.microsoft/create/en/blog/best-resume-fonts/ "The best resume fonts, sizes, and formatting tips (2026) | Microsoft Word Blog"))
    
- Consistent spacing and margins. ([Center for Career Development](https://careerdevelopment.princeton.edu/resume-guide/basic-principles-resume-writing "Basic Principles of Resume Writing | Center for Career Development"))
    
- Black or very dark body text on white/light background. This is a practical readability synthesis consistent with the cited layout guidance. ([Center for Career Development](https://careerdevelopment.princeton.edu/resume-guide/basic-principles-resume-writing "Basic Principles of Resume Writing | Center for Career Development"))
    

## C. Section naming rules

- Name and contact details at top of body. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/200989175-Unsuccessful-resume-parse "Unsuccessful resume parse – Greenhouse Support"))
    
- Use standard labels. ([Utah Valley University](https://www.uvu.edu/career-internship/docs/career-services/creating-ats-friendly-resumes.pdf "Crack the Code: Creating ATS Friendly Resumes"))
    
- Experience section should literally include the word **Experience** somewhere in the heading. ([Utah Valley University](https://www.uvu.edu/career-internship/docs/career-services/creating-ats-friendly-resumes.pdf "Crack the Code: Creating ATS Friendly Resumes"))
    
- Skills section should exist if the role depends on explicit tools/skills. ([Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume-template "How To Write an ATS Resume (With Template and Tips) | Indeed.com"))
    
- Education section should use standard degree/institution formatting. ([Utah Valley University](https://www.uvu.edu/career-internship/docs/career-services/creating-ats-friendly-resumes.pdf "Crack the Code: Creating ATS Friendly Resumes"))
    

## D. Content and keyword rules

- Mirror the target title where truthful. This is a resume-optimization inference grounded in the keyword-match guidance. ([Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume-template "How To Write an ATS Resume (With Template and Tips) | Indeed.com"))
    
- Include hard skills from the posting exactly as written where applicable. ([Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume-template "How To Write an ATS Resume (With Template and Tips) | Indeed.com"))
    
- Include certifications/licences using exact official names. ([Utah Valley University](https://www.uvu.edu/career-internship/docs/career-services/creating-ats-friendly-resumes.pdf "Crack the Code: Creating ATS Friendly Resumes"))
    
- Include acronyms plus expanded forms. ([Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume-template "How To Write an ATS Resume (With Template and Tips) | Indeed.com"))
    
- Use keywords naturally; no stuffing, hidden text, or pasted job description. ([Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume-template "How To Write an ATS Resume (With Template and Tips) | Indeed.com"))
    
- Show keywords in context through achievements, not just a list. ([Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume-template "How To Write an ATS Resume (With Template and Tips) | Indeed.com"))
    

## E. Language and typo rules

- No spelling errors. ([Utah Valley University](https://www.uvu.edu/career-internship/docs/career-services/creating-ats-friendly-resumes.pdf "Crack the Code: Creating ATS Friendly Resumes"))
    
- No grammar errors. ([Utah Valley University](https://www.uvu.edu/career-internship/docs/career-services/creating-ats-friendly-resumes.pdf "Crack the Code: Creating ATS Friendly Resumes"))
    
- Consistent date format throughout.
    
- Consistent punctuation style throughout.
    
- Consistent capitalization style throughout.
    
- No broken line wrapping that separates role/company/date relationships.
    
- No unexplained abbreviations in job titles, schools, certifications, or core skills. ([Greenhouse Support](https://support.greenhouse.io/hc/en-us/articles/200989175-Unsuccessful-resume-parse "Unsuccessful resume parse – Greenhouse Support"))

# Copy-paste prompt for another LLM

Use this exactly:

```text
You are auditing a CV/résumé for maximum ATS compatibility and recruiter readability.

Your job is to score the document using the rubric below and then rewrite or recommend fixes.

Important rules:
1. Assume there is no universal ATS standard, so optimize for the safest common denominator.
2. Prioritize parser safety first, then keyword match, then recruiter readability.
3. If any automatic-fail blocker is present, cap the score at 69 until fixed.
4. Be strict.

AUTOMATIC-FAIL BLOCKERS:
- scanned/image-based PDF
- tables
- text boxes
- headers/footers containing important info
- multi-column/sidebar layout
- graphics/icons/photos/charts/progress bars/logos carrying meaning
- unsupported file format or ignored application instructions
- creative/nonstandard section names
- contact info hidden in header/footer/text box
- serious typos in skills, titles, company names, certifications, or dates

WEIGHTED RUBRIC (100):
1. File format and parse integrity — 20
2. Layout and graphic design safety — 20
3. Typography and readability — 15
4. Section structure and data extraction clarity — 20
5. Keyword alignment to the target role — 15
6. Content quality and recruiter readability — 10

SCORING CRITERIA:
FILE FORMAT / PARSE:
- correct file type for employer request
- DOCX or text-based PDF if no specific instruction
- text selectable and copy-pastes in correct order
- no corruption, missing bullets, strange symbols, or jumbled sections
- lightweight professional file

LAYOUT / GRAPHIC DESIGN:
- single-column only
- no tables, text boxes, sidebars, columns
- no photos, icons, charts, logos, graphics, progress bars
- no important info in headers/footers
- clean spacing, simple visual hierarchy, standard margins

TYPOGRAPHY:
- standard font only
- body 10–12 pt
- headings clearly larger
- limited font variation
- no decorative fonts
- no underline/excessive italics for important info
- consistent spacing and alignment

SECTION STRUCTURE:
- standard headings only: Summary, Skills, Experience, Education, Certifications, Projects, Volunteer
- Experience section includes the word “Experience”
- reverse chronological order
- each job entry clearly shows title, employer, dates, location if used
- education clearly shows degree, field, institution, date

KEYWORDS:
- matches the target job description
- uses exact hard-skill keywords naturally
- includes exact titles, tools, certifications, and relevant phrases
- includes acronym + full spelling where relevant
- no keyword stuffing or hidden text

CONTENT QUALITY:
- concise bullet points
- strong action verbs
- measurable achievements/results
- relevant content only
- no spelling/grammar mistakes
- consistent date, punctuation, and capitalization style

OUTPUT FORMAT:
A. Overall ATS score: X/100
B. Blockers found: yes/no
C. Category scores with 1–2 sentence justification each
D. Exact issues found, grouped by:
   - parsing risk
   - design/layout
   - typography
   - sections/structure
   - keywords
   - content/clarity
   - typos/grammar
E. Priority fixes in this order:
   1. critical blockers
   2. high-impact ATS fixes
   3. recruiter-readability upgrades
F. Final verdict:
   - Excellent
   - Strong
   - Risky
   - Not ATS-compliant

Be explicit, tough, and practical.
```

# Best-practice verdict the LLM should use

A CV/résumé should be considered **“ATS excellent”** only if it is:

- **Plain enough for a parser**
    
- **Specific enough for keyword matching**
    
- **Clean enough for a human recruiter to scan in seconds** ([Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume-template "How To Write an ATS Resume (With Template and Tips) | Indeed.com"))
