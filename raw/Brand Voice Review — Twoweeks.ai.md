# THE ONE THING WORKING BEST

The **"X. Y."** panel-head pattern is your signature move: _APPLY. MOVE. · TRACK. DON'T CHASE. · CLOSE. DONE._ Guard it. Don't dilute with 3-word heads, don't soften with adjectives in the body. When a panel head doesn't fit the two-period rhythm (Panels 1, 2, 5, 11), you can see the copy wobble.

That pattern + the values-row juxtaposition (Elite Indifference / Friday afternoon) are the two artifacts worth treating as brand IP.
