For French:
- no space before comma or period
- non-breaking space before : ; ? !
- use « »
- keep accents on capitals
- use comma decimals
- space between number and unit

For English:
- no French spacing before punctuation
- use curly quotes
- choose US or UK and stay consistent
- use period/comma placement according to chosen variant
- use month names in dates
- space between number and SI unit

Global rule:
Never mix French and English typographic conventions in the same passage unless explicitly requested.
